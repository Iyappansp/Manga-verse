Synopsis

In a world where gates connect reality to dungeons filled with monsters, ordinary people awakened with powers are known as "Hunters." Sung Jin-Woo is known as the weakest hunter of all mankind. One day, after surviving a horrific double dungeon, he awakens with a mysterious system that allows him to level up indefinitely.
