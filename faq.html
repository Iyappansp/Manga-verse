Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about reading, translating, and subscriptions on MangaVerse.

How do I become a translator?

Head over to the registration page and select "Apply as Translator". You will need to take a short proficiency test in your chosen language pair before you can join a project team.

Are the translations official?

We host both official publisher releases and high-quality community scanlations. Official releases are clearly marked with a green checkmark badge in the library.

Can I download chapters to read offline?

Offline reading is currently available exclusively to Premium Supporters. Once subscribed, a download button will appear on the chapter reader interface.

How do I switch to Dark Mode or RTL?

You can easily customize your experience using the toggles located in the top navigation bar. Click the moon/sun icon for theme switching, and the RTL button for right-to-left reading mode.
